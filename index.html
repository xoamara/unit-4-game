Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. When you click on the crystals individually it adds to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your score exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. When you restart the game the value of the crystals change.
